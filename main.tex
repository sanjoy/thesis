%	ABSTRACT PAGE

\addtotoc{Abstract}

\abstract{\addtocontents{toc}{\vspace{1em}}

The advent of large computing clusters and mobile devices has made
reducing the energy consumed by CPU-intensive computation an important
problem in compiler design.  Our work revolves around a tool to
estimate the energy consumption profile of an application,\wattage,
and explores the possibility compilers optimizing programs for energy
efficiency.

}
